About Thegames.com

Welcome to the Thegames network! Our sites got started way back in 1997 to make gaming enjoyable for people who thought it couldn't be fun. We're happy to report that we've convinced millions of people over the years that gaming + reviews is an equation that makes sense.

Here's a quick rundown of what our site offers.

  • Latest news and ongoings in gaming world.
  • New Games introduced for the hardcore gamers.
  • A different section for genres in gaming.
  • Accounts for users to follow up latest updates and notifications from TheGames.com
